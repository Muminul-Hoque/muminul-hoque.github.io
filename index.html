About Me

I am Muhammed Muminul Hoque, an aspiring researcher with strong interests in privacy-preserving technologies, secure systems, and trustworthy AI. I completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of Dhaka, building a foundation in systems, security, and applied machine learning. I am now preparing to pursue a Ph.D., aiming to design resilient, privacy-aware systems that strengthen trust in digital platforms.

My research interests lie at the intersection of cybersecurity, privacy, and intelligent systems. I am particularly motivated by challenges in digital identity protection, secure AI for low-resource environments, phishing detection with adaptive LLM pipelines, and usability-focused explainability methods.
