Adaptive Phishing Email Detection via Multiple LLMs: A Dynamic Keyword Based Approach (2025 - Ongoing)

  • Developing a multiple-LLM pipeline (ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, LLaMA, Grok) for extracting phishing indicators from emails.

  • Supports real-time detection in multiple-source environments (local inbox, Gmail, datasets) with robust risk scoring.
